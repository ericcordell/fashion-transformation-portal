✅ Requirements for Portal Inclusion

Primary Requirement: LLTT Label

Your OPIF MUST include the label LLTT to appear in this portal.

This is the primary filter that determines whether an OPIF is tracked in the E2E Fashion Portal.

How to Add the LLTT Label:

  1. Open your OPIF in Jira (jira.walmart.com)
  2. Look for the "Labels" field (usually in the right sidebar)
  3. Click "+ Add" or click on the Labels field
  4. Type LLTT and press Enter
  5. Save the OPIF

🔄 Workstream Assignment Logic

Important: The workstream (Strategy, Design, Buying, Allocation) displayed in the portal is NOT directly determined by the Activity Type field.

🧠 How Workstreams Are Assigned:

The portal analyzes the entire OPIF content including epic name, description, labels, and context to intelligently assign the appropriate workstream based on predefined business rules.

Workstream Categories:

📊 Strategy

Strategic initiatives, planning, forecasting, and transformation programs that span multiple workstreams

🎨 Design

Product design, UX/UI, PLM (Centric), conceptualization, and creative development activities

🛒 Buying

Procurement, vendor management, buy planning (BPE/DBP), assortment planning, and purchasing

📦 Allocation

Distribution, inventory management, allocation planning (AEX), and fulfillment optimization

📌 Note on Activity Type:

The Activity Type field in your OPIF is one of many signals used for workstream assignment, but it's not the sole determinant. The portal uses intelligent inference based on the overall OPIF context.

If your OPIF's workstream assignment seems incorrect, please contact the E2E Fashion Portal support team (see "Need Help?" section).

🔧 Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions:

Q: My OPIF isn't showing up in the portal

A: Check the following:

  1. Verify the LLTT label is added to your OPIF
  2. Wait 24 hours for the next automated sync
  3. Ensure your OPIF is in an active status (not Closed/Done)
  4. Check that your OPIF appears on the Confluence dashboard

Q: My status/date is outdated in the portal

A: The portal syncs daily from Confluence. Updates made in Jira will appear in the portal within 24 hours after the next automated sync runs (typically early morning).

Q: Which field should I update to change my status?

A: Update BOTH fields:

  • Status Color (Green/Yellow/Red) — visible in the OPIF sidebar
  • Ready to Start Field in the top header (Ready to Start, In Progress, Blocked, etc.)

Q: My Assignee and Product Manager don't match

A: They should be the same person. Update the Assignee field to match your Product Manager for consistency. The portal uses the Assignee field as the primary owner.

Q: How do I change which workstream my OPIF appears in?

A: Update the Activity Type field in your OPIF to the appropriate category (Strategy, Design, Buying, or Allocation related activity).

🔄 Sync Schedule

Understanding when your updates will appear in the portal:

📅

Daily Automated Sync

The portal automatically syncs with Confluence every business day (Monday - Friday).

⏰ Changes you make in Jira today will appear in the portal the next business day morning.

📌 Weekend updates will appear on Monday morning.

⚡

Data Flow

Jira OPIF → Confluence Dashboard → E2E Fashion Portal

📌 Make sure your OPIF is visible on the Confluence dashboard to ensure it syncs to the portal.
